How TaskoBid Works

Get work done in 3 simple steps

Step 1

Post Your Task

Open the TaskoBid app and describe what you need. Be as simple or detailed as you want:

  • "Need AC repair tomorrow morning"
  • "Looking for a painter for 2 rooms"
  • "Plumber needed - kitchen tap leaking"

Add photos if helpful. Set your budget preference. Choose your location and timing.

Step 2

Receive Competitive Bids

Within minutes, verified professionals near you start bidding:

  • See their quoted price upfront
  • Check estimated completion time
  • Read their ratings and reviews
  • View their work history and portfolio

Compare offers side-by-side. Chat directly through the app to clarify details.

Step 3

Choose & Complete

Pick the provider that fits your needs best:

  • Accept the bid you prefer
  • Provider gets notified instantly
  • Work begins at scheduled time
  • Track progress through updates
  • Pay securely after completion

Rate your experience. Help others make informed choices.

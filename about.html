About TaskoBid

Revolutionizing how India hires service professionals

Our Story

TaskoBid was born from a simple frustration: finding reliable service professionals shouldn't be this hard.

We noticed that people waste hours calling multiple providers, comparing prices over phone calls, and often ending up with inflated quotes or unreliable service. Meanwhile, skilled professionals struggle to find consistent work and often rely on expensive middlemen.

So we asked: What if we could flip this model? What if professionals competed for your work, giving you the power to choose based on price, ratings, and availability?

TaskoBid was created to bridge this gap. A platform where transparency meets convenience. Where professionals get direct access to customers. Where users get the best price without the hassle.

Today, we're proud to connect thousands of users with verified professionals across India, making home services simple, affordable, and reliable.
